🎚️

One-button capture

Records your mic and the computer's meeting audio (Zoom, Meet, Teams — anything) natively. No bot in your call.

🗣️

Who-said-what transcripts

Whisper transcription with speaker separation. "You" is labeled automatically; everyone else gets told apart on-device.

📋

Structured notes, not word-soup

Executive summary, key decisions, action items with owners and checkboxes. Rendered as cards you can actually scan.

✍️

Scratchpad + enhance

Jot rough bullets during the meeting. AI expands each line with the decisions, numbers, and quotes from the transcript.

💬

Ask your meeting anything

"What did we agree about pricing?" Chat with any transcript — answered locally by an on-device LLM.

📧

One-click follow-up email

Recap email with decisions and action items, grounded in the transcript. Plus templates, search, auto-titles, Markdown export.

"Unsigned app? Why should I trust you?"

Because you don't have to. The entire source code is public. Read it, build it yourself, or watch its network traffic — after the one-time model download, there isn't any. A $99 Apple certificate proves someone paid Apple. Open source proves what the app actually does.

Prefer zero security dialogs? Install via Homebrew:
