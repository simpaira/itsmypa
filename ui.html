Where do I find this?
Google Calendar
  1. In the left sidebar, under "My calendars", hover your calendar name
  2. Click the ⋮ menu next to it → Settings and sharing
  3. Scroll to Integrate calendar → copy Secret address in iCal format
Outlook.com
  1. Settings (gear icon) → Calendar → Shared calendars
  2. Under Publish a calendar, pick your calendar and a permission level, then click Publish
  3. Copy the ICS link (not the HTML one)
Apple Calendar (iCloud)
  1. Right-click / Control-click the calendar in the sidebar → Share Calendar
  2. Turn on Public Calendar → copy the link, then change webcal:// to https:// if needed

Welcome to ItsMyPA

Private by design. Recording, transcription, speaker ID and AI notes all run on this device. Nothing is uploaded, ever.
One button, both sides. Press record and ItsMyPA captures you and everyone else on the call — Zoom, Meet, Teams, any app — automatically.
Notes you can act on. Decisions, action items and a follow-up email draft — and “Ask AI” answers questions from the transcript.

Two quick permissions

MicrophoneSo ItsMyPA can hear you. Required to record.
Computer audioCaptures the other people on video calls. Wear headphones on calls — otherwise their voices bleed into your mic and speaker labels get muddy. Optional — skip if you only record in person.
